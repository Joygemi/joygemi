# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 8, 2026  
**Platform:** JoyGemi.com  
**Applies to:** All visitors to JoyGemi.com

---

## 1. Overview

JoyGemi.com is a browser-based gaming platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site, how we use it, what third parties receive it, and what controls you have.

JoyGemi.com is provided for entertainment only. Players do not earn money or financial rewards from gameplay. Any advertising revenue supports the operation and development of JoyGemi.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected

When you visit JoyGemi.com, we automatically receive basic technical information from your browser:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Language preference
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URL (the page you came from)

This information is collected via Google Analytics and standard server logs.

### Provided by You

If you choose to contact us via the contact form on the site, we collect the information you voluntarily submit (such as your name and email address).

If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account's display name, email address, and profile photo URL via Firebase Authentication. We do not receive your Google password.

### Cookies and Local Storage

JoyGemi uses browser cookies and `localStorage` to:

- Remember your dark/light theme preference.
- Store your cookie consent decision.
- Persist your list of favourited games (keyed by Firebase UID if signed in).
- Track recently played games in the current session.

Third-party cookies are set by Google AdSense and Google Analytics. See Section 5 for details.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Operate, maintain, and improve the JoyGemi platform.
- Load and deliver browser games correctly across devices.
- Analyse traffic patterns, popular games, and technical issues.
- Display contextually relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.
- Personalise your experience (theme preference, favourites, recently played).
- Respond to contact form enquiries.
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

JoyGemi displays advertisements powered by Google AdSense (`pub-7608862327127348`). Google uses cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on your prior visits to JoyGemi.com and other websites.

Games embedded from partner sites (e.g. madkidgames.com) are loaded in an `<iframe>`. Those partner sites may set their own cookies and collect their own usage data, subject to their own privacy policies. JoyGemi does not control partner data practices.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as described in Section 5 (with Google services) and only to the extent necessary to operate the platform.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Analytics data** is retained in GA4 for 14 months (Google's default).
- **Contact form submissions** are retained for up to 12 months for support purposes.
- **Firebase Auth data** (your Google account association) is retained until you delete your account or we delete it.
- **localStorage data** (theme, favourites) is stored on your device indefinitely until you clear your browser data.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate personal data.
- **Delete** your personal data.
- **Object** to or restrict certain processing.
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (where processing is based on consent).

To exercise these rights, contact us via the form at [joygemi.com/#contact](https://joygemi.com/#contact).

### Cookie Controls

You can manage cookies in your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Blocking cookies may affect your ability to save favourites and theme preferences.
